"Jeu de memoire"

*is a game involving a human player and a machine on an N*N grid with diagonals. 


*It has two phases: memorization and prediction. 


*Players must remember diagonal orientations and predict the ball's path, which changes upon touching diagonals.
*The game offers a main menu, score display, high scores, and multiple levels with advancing difficulty. 


*It combines memory and puzzle elements, making it engaging and strategic for players.
